About The Event

Pradyut Parva 2 is the flagship technical symposium hosted by the IEEE Student Branch of Sairam College of Engineering. It's a two-day extravaganza celebrating technology, innovation, and collaborative spirit. We bring together the brightest minds from across the nation to compete, learn, and network. Our mission is to provide a platform for students to showcase their technical prowess, engage in challenging competitions, and get inspired by the latest trends in the industry.
